MTN Mobile Money Transfer

Riches Bank partnered with MTN Uganda to provide the mobile money service.

MTN MobileMoney is a service that enables people to send and receive money anywhere in Uganda using their phones.

The service operates by using an agent who is the intermediary between the parties in the transaction.

The Bank has signed as ordinary and super agents to serve individuals and MTN agents respectively.
