Bank Over Draft

Very short term credit facility designed to meet the Bank customers' urgent day-to-day cash requirements for their business transactions. The facility is renewable based on borrower's credit history with the Bank.

Product Features:

• Loan term is up to a maximum of 12 months

• Commitment fee of 2% is charged

• Repayable through good account deposits

• Interest is linked to the Bank’s prime lending rate and charged on declining balance

• Minimum amount offered is Ushs100,000

Basic Requirements:

Current account with the bank.

Application fee of UShs. 15,000.

Completed Overdraft facility application form.

Collateral for securing the loan.

• Financial card required

Benefits:

Faster processing speed.

Available funds for emergency cash needs.

Multiple disbursements allowed.

Offers greater financial flexibility.
