Cente Fixed Deposit Account

An account where a customer's deposit are fixed for an agreed period of time subject to no withdraws

before the elapse of period. Income will be forfeirted for cash withdraws made before maturity period.

Main features

. Minimum amount to be fixed is Ushs,200000

. Maximum amount to be fixed is open

. Can be held in both local and foreign currency

.Cash withdraws personally made to the account by holders

Benefits

An account earns interest at a rate higher than that applied on saving account

.Account can be liquidated anytime on notice

. Account can be pledged as collateral for loans

. Renewable on maturity of 2,4,12 months

. Rates are negotiable
